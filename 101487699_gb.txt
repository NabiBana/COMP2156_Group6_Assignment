Overview:

George Brown College (GBC) is a well-known public college of applied arts and technology located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
Established in 1967, it is one of the largest and most respected colleges in the country,
offering a wide range of diplomas, certificates, degrees, and postgraduate programs in various fields.
