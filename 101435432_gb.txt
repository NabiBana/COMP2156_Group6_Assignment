George Brown College (GBC) is a public college in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
It offers a wide range of programs in business, technology, health sciences, 
and creative arts. The college emphasizes hands-on learning and industry 
connections, providing students with practical experience through internships 
and co-op placements.

GBC has three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront. It is 
renowned for its close partnerships with industry leaders, ensuring students 
gain real-world skills that align with job market demands.
