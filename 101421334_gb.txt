Info about George Brown College
George Brown College has a variety of interesting programs which will help you get prepared with all the knowledge you need for your future career.
Students from George Brown College can directly get jobs based on the name of the college! They do not look at there grades.
The Waterfront campus at George Brown College is considered one of the most beautiful campuses in Canada because the back of the building faces Lake Ontario, and the classrooms and some areas can get a really great view.
Alot of colleges have there campuses spread out, but all George Brown college campuses are in Downtown, Toronto which can be very easy for studdents to go from campus to campus and there are also subway stations close to each campus for transportation.
